    Procedure to Join at IITK Climbing

    Day pass

    You can visit us at the wall when there is a climbing session going on. If there is a room, the instructor will ask you to pay for the day pass. Pay the day fee (payment procedure given below) and try climbing once.

    Permanent slot

    You can email us about the availability of a slot. If a slot is vacant, you will receive an email with a deadline. Please pay before the deadline (payment procedure below) and send the receipt and filled out waiver form back. No direct payment (without an email from us) will be entertained.

    Special events

    You can also participate in one of the regularly organized climbing workshops (though they are sold out pretty quickly), generally the email is sent once in two months.

    If you want to try out once, you can get a day pass. If you are interested, you can get a permanent slot.

    1. Login to SBI-I Collect.
    2. After selecting Uttar Pradesh then Educational Institutions and then Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, Select PHYSICAL EDUCATION SECTION RECEIPT.

    5. Send the receipt and filled out waiver form to Pushpendra Singh (climbing.iitk@gmail.com).
    6. Don't pay in any other section. It will not be counted.

    Timings for climbing sessions

    Timings for climbing sessions: We have sessions every evening and most mornings. The evening sessions run from 6pm onwards. Morning sessions depend on the time of the year and can range from 5:30-7:30 in summers to 7:00-8:30 in winters. Please note that if you are absent in your slot for multiple days without notification, your slot will be given to the next person.

    About Climbing

    Rock climbing, in the modern sense, is the art of scaling steep ascents using all kinds and sizes of hand and foot holds. It is practiced on a wall with many features and varying angles. The efforts of climbers have transformed sports climbing into a growing, global sport (around 75 countries participate in the world cup), getting widespread attention after inclusion in Tokyo Olympics 2020. There are good reasons to climb too, it has been consistently rated as one of the top healthiest sports. We put safety as our first priority, and strongly believe that proper climbing is safe climbing. Our coach, Mr. Pushpender Singh, is a national level climber and provides training to newcomers in climbing skills as well as safety procedures.
